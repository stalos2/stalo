About Us

St. Aloysius Senior Secondary School Nabbingo is a mixed day and boarding Catholic-founded school offering Arts and Sciences at O-Level and A-Level. Located at Kigwanya-Bataka, 8 miles off Kampala-Masaka Road, UNEB Registration Number U0653.

Our mission is to provide holistic education that nurtures academic excellence, moral integrity, and social responsibility.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution that nurtures future leaders through innovation and excellence.
